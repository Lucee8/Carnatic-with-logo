Our Legacy

A symphony of spices,
crafted over decades.


Founded on the uncompromising principles of traditional South Indian cooking, Carnatic Military Hotel is more than a restaurant — it's a custodian of authentic flavors.

We believe that true luxury lies in the purity of ingredients. Our spices are hand-pounded, our meats are slow-cooked in copper vessels, and every dish is prepared with the exact rigor of our ancestral kitchens.

"We don't just serve food; we serve memories forged in the heat of a wood-fired stove."
